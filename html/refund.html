Refunds and Returns Policy

Last Updated: 5th February 2025

Thank you for shopping with Ascertain Systems. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

1. Returns

1.1 Eligibility for Returns

Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery under the following conditions:

  • Item is unused, undamaged, and in original packaging
  • Includes all accessories, manuals, and documentation
  • Not listed as non-returnable (see 1.3)

1.2 Return Process

To return a product:

  1. Email karthikgibbi@gmail.com with order number and reason
  2. If approved, we will provide return instructions
  3. You are responsible for shipping unless item is defective/damaged
  4. We inspect and notify you of refund approval

1.3 Non-Returnable Products

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Used/opened medical devices (for hygiene reasons)
  • Digital/downloadable databases
  • Customized medical equipment
  • Items marked Final Sale or Non-Returnable
