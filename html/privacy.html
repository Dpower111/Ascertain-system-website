Welcome to Ascertain Systems ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy.

This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit www.ascertainsystems.com ("Website") and use our medical devices and medical database solutions.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number, and postal address
  • Payment and billing details for purchases
  • Account credentials (if applicable)

1.2 Medical Data (HIPAA Compliant)

  • ECG and heart-related health data processed through our medical devices

  • Data is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with HIPAA

1.3 Technical & Usage Data

  • IP address, browser type, and device information

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To Provide Services: Process transactions, deliver medical devices, and manage accounts
  • To Improve Our Website: Analyze usage trends to enhance user experience

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill orders and provide requested services
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws

  • Consent: When you opt-in to marketing or submit health data

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

5. Cookies & Tracking

Manage preferences via browser settings or our cookie consent banner.
